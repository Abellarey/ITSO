About ITSO

Who We Are

The Information Technology Student Organization (ITSO) is a community of passionate students dedicated to exploring the vast world of technology. We provide a platform for students to connect, learn, and grow together in the field of information technology.

Our organization brings together students from various IT disciplines to collaborate on projects, share knowledge, and prepare for careers in the ever-evolving tech industry.
